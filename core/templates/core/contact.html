Introduction

Hello and welcome!

I´m María, the brains behind Little Dream Gardens. I´m passionate about all crafts, especially all miniature related, and I started miniature gardening in 2020 and it has been an amazing journey! I wanted to share all that I´ve been learning during this past year, in combination with my new projects and diy´s.

Is funny because when I was little, my mum use to check my pockets when I came back home from the playground. There, she will find innumerable treasures such as twigs, pebbles, leafs or candy wraps… now, a few years later, I still come back home with my pockets full of new things for my miniature gardens.

Please bare in mind that all my tutorials are for adults, unless otherwise stated. The props I make are not toys and are not safe for children, so is better to keep them away from them.

Feel free to contact me with any questions and to send me your creations! I hope you enjoy my website!
